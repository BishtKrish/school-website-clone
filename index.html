About G.H.S.S Dungra

G.H.S.S. DUNGRA was established in 2002 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in DHAULADEVI block of ALMORA district of Uttarakhand. The school consists of Grades from NC to 10.The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Hindi and English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April. The school has Private building. It has got 12 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has No Boundary Wall boundary wall. The school has have electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Water Tank and it is functional. The school has 2 boys toilet and it is functional. and 2 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has one playground. The school has a library and has 150 books in its library. The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The school has 10 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school is having a computer aided learning lab. All about this school it is very best school in rural area.
